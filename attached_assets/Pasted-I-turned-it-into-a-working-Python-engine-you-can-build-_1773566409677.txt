The core logic

This is the architecture:

Company Quality = business strength

Stock Opportunity = attractiveness of the stock

Expectation = how much success is already priced in

Mispricing = evidence the market is wrong

Fragility = how easily the thesis can break

Position size = function of all the above

So it stops the system from overbuying “great but fully priced” companies and also avoids loading up on “cheap but fragile” names.
