The Missing Piece: Factor Regime Weighting

Your current architecture likely assumes:

Factors → Score → Buy/Sell

But markets change regimes.

What works in one environment fails in another.

Example:

Market Regime	Best Factors
Bull market	Momentum & growth
Bear market	Quality & cash flow
Rising rates	profitability
Falling rates	innovation & growth

Without regime awareness, models misfire badly.

What This Means in Practice

A company like Adobe may have excellent fundamentals but still perform poorly if the macro regime penalizes software valuations.

Your current system already hints at this:

Market Cycle: Bear Cycle
MA50 < MA200
Avoid new entries

That’s regime detection, but it's not yet controlling factor weights.

How to Implement This (Simple Version)

Create a new module:

market_regime.ts

It determines regime using signals like:

SP500 trend
Volatility index
Market breadth
Interest rates

Example logic:

if (sp500_ma50 > sp500_ma200) regime = "bull"
else regime = "bear"
Then Adjust Engine Weights

Instead of fixed engine weights:

composite =
0.4 * fortress +
0.35 * rocket +
0.25 * wave

Use:

weights = getRegimeWeights(regime)

composite =
weights.fortress * fortress +
weights.rocket * rocket +
weights.wave * wave

Example:

Bull Market:
Fortress 25%
Rocket 40%
Wave 35%

Bear Market:
Fortress 50%
Rocket 20%
Wave 30%
